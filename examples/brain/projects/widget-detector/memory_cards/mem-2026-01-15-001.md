# Occlusion-augmentation curriculum beats backbone scaling on synth-widgets (+4.1 vs +0.6 mAP)

## Claim
On synth-widgets-1k, the v2 occlusion-augmentation curriculum improves mAP by +4.1 at
baseline compute, while a 3× backbone gives only +0.6 at 3× compute.

## Evidence
- experiments/occ-curriculum-v2.md final table: baseline 67.2 → curriculum 71.3 mAP.
- experiments/backbone-scale-v1.md: baseline 67.2 → 67.8 mAP at 3× FLOPs.

## Implication
Prefer the augmentation curriculum over backbone scaling for this task. Do not reopen the
backbone-scaling line without a new reason.
